Outsourced DPO-as-a-Service (Data protection Officer)

XpertDPO can provide everything from an interim DPO to a full Outsourced Data Protection Officer Service.
Each service provides crucial expertise and an affordable alternative to employing a privacy expert to ensure ‘smart compliance’ with the GDPR.

Outsourced DPO-as-a-Service (Data protection Officer) Package

As part of our GDPR service portfolio, our qualified and experienced consultants will advise, inform and support you in all aspects of your organisation’s requirements of a Data Protection Officer. On-going monitoring and review of your organisation’s processes and procedures in relation to its position as data controller and data processor, and reporting and advising on compliance on a regular basis is an important aspect of this role.

Key elements of our Outsourced DPO service include:

Quarterly reports to senior management: XpertDPO will prepare and deliver quarterly reports to the senior management that detail:

  • Any ongoing internal Data Protection related issues;
  • Detail and analyse any Data Breaches;
  • Overview of current compliance levels;
  • Number of Subject Access Requests processed in period with detailed analysis;
  • Any external / general Data Protection updates deemed necessary to raise;

Ongoing compliance monitoring: XpertDPO’s outsourced service includes the provision of ongoing compliance monitoring. Complying with the GDPR doesn’t stop when the policies and procedures have been finalised. Your organisation will need to ensure that those policies and procedures are adhered to and XpertDPO will provide this service. Our Centre of Excellence monitors ongoing legal and regulatory decisions in relation to all matter data protection, so you can be assured that the advice that we provide is based on current case law.

Data Subject Access Requests: XpertDPO will support your organisation to plan, design and implement effective procedures and processes to respond to data subject requests including to address the right of access, right to be forgotten, right to portability and right to objection/restriction. XpertDPO will also provide real-time support during a request and assist in or oversee the response process. Understanding Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) requirements: XpertDPO can run workshop support to identify and review key data flows, proposed changes or projects, perform screening of proposed projects or processing activities to determine whether DPIAs are necessary in line with the GDPR, and to monitor ongoing DPIAs. It is important to note that it is not always the first point of contact that triggers a DPIA. We must analyse the whole lifecycle of the data across teams and departments.

Data protection awareness training: XpertDPO can provide a number of training options that will empower and educate your management and staff to fully understand their obligations in relation to data protection, including clinic type sessions & classroom based training certified to QQI standard. These training options will be fully tailored and customised to meet the needs of of your organisation.

Communication with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner: XpertDPO will register as the DPO for your organisation and we will act as the main point of contact with the ODPC.
